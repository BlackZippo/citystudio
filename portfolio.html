Portfolio

CityStudio predstavlja tim stručnjaka koji pruža kompletnu podršku, od produkcije i tehničke asistencije do regulisanja neophodnih dozola za različitihe projekte. Organizujemo kompleksne, tailor-made događaje, kreirane po meri vaših ciljeva, uz tim koji razume svaku fazu procesa – od prve skice do finalnog rezultata.

200th Anniversary — Erste Bank

Novak Djoković – 311 Weeks as World No. 1

A powerful 3D mapping celebrated the GOAT and his record-breaking 311 weeks at the top of the ATP rankings.

BELEF – A City as a Stage

Creative Concept & Visual Identity— logo, campaign visuals, animations, and a city-as-stage concept. Spatial Design & Production Programs were staged across Kalemegdan Fortress, throughout urban open-air spots, and in the magical setup of Botanical Garden with multiple stages. Each edition explored how space, sound, and audience interaction can redefine the cultural experience.

Film Center Serbia – Events & International Presence

We supported Film Center Serbia with event production, media strategy, and international positioning. This included the official event for the film Russian Consul, the organization of the annual New Year’s reception, year-round media support, and the creation of a presentation brochure for the Cannes Film Festival.

BDP – A New Era of Theater

Our team led the complete rebranding of Belgrade Drama Theatre — from logo and visual identity to a bold new creative direction. Thanks to this transformation, BDP reestablished itself as a cultural force in both Belgrade and the region. We also created the identity of the RUTA Festival, giving shape to a new platform connecting the most prominent theaters across the Balkans. We didn’t just change how BDP looks — we changed where it stands.

CORONA VS CULTURE – Regional Voices, Global Pause

When culture stood still, we started the conversation. During the pandemic, we launched a regional conference that connected cultural workers across borders — live, online, and in real time.

CULTURE VS CORONA – Survival of art after pandemic

Held for two years in a row, it opened space for real dialogue when it was needed most.

Legacy in Light – Stefan Nemanja

In honor of the founder of the Nemanjić dynasty, a striking 4D mapping brought the story of Serbian statehood to life — a light-powered tribute to the roots of a nation.

75 Years of Innovation – Jaroslav Černi Institute

A multimedia event with holograms, choreography, and bold visual storytelling — honoring the region’s leading water management institute. We led full production, PR and creative direction.
